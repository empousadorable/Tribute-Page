Steve Irwin

Crocodile Hunter. Wildlife Warrior

Here are some facts about Steve Irwin and his life

  • Steve Irwin grew up at a zoo
  • Steve Irwin met his wife, Terri, at the Australia Zoo
  • The first episode of The Crocodile Hunter was filmed during Steve and Terri Irwin's honeymoon
  • Irwin believed his enthusiasm was the key to The Crocodile Hunter's success
  • The show's popularity spread far beyond America
  • Creating awareness about conservationism was the show's main goal
  • The Crocodile Hunter's popularity allowed Irwin to expand the Australia Zoo
  • Irwin was no stranger to being bitten by the animals he handled, and knew it made for great television
  • Irwin caused a major controversy when he fed a crocodile while holding his infant son
  • Parrots were the one animal that terrified Irwin
  • He discovered a new species of turtle
  • There's a snail named after Irwin and his famous catchphrase
  • Robert Irwin is following in his father's footsteps

"I have no fear of losing my life - if I have to save a koala or a crocodile or a kangaroo or a snake, mate, I will save it."

-- Steve Irwin
